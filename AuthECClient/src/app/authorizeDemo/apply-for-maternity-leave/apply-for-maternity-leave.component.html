📌 Policy Overview

Our institution provides up to 90 days of maternity leave to eligible staff. All requests must be submitted at least 30 days in advance and must include medical documentation.

  • Leave Duration: Up to 90 days
  • Eligible: Full-time staff with 1+ year of service
  • Paid Leave: Yes
